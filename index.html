About the App

AKU Pharmacy Residency is a mobile application designed to support pharmacy residents at Aga Khan University. The app provides access to educational resources, clinical guidelines, drug information, and learning experience tracking — all in one place.

Built by students at AKU's pharmacy residency program, with help of 1PercentLabs.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication and account identification
  • Name — used to personalize your experience within the app

When you use the app, we automatically collect:

  • PDF access logs — records of which resources you accessed and when, to support your learning progress
  • Learning experience data — information you voluntarily enter to track your residency activities

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and provide access to the app
  • To deliver educational content and resources relevant to your program
  • To track and display your learning progress
  • To improve the app experience

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on cloud-hosted servers with strict access control policies ensuring that users can only access their own data. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

PDF resources are stored in private storage with signed, time-limited URLs to prevent unauthorized access.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is used solely for the purposes described in this policy.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party cloud services for authentication, data storage, and content delivery. These services are bound by their own privacy policies and are used solely to operate the app.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for pharmacy residents and healthcare professionals. It is not directed at children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
